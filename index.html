What does DNR stand for?

DNR.org are an organization built up of volunteer higher-education students, educators, and policymakers

Our goal is to completely reverse, immediately cease, and set further precedent against the banning of books and censorship of knowledge in public schools and libraries

Teacher and student acvocacy combined with close working relationships with both grassroots organizations like SC for Ed and the ACLU.

Uncensored, transparent education is an absolute necessity in an age where partisanship promotes the categoric erasal of identities and resources pertaining to disaffected minorities and those not aligned with the status quo. As such, we must campaign for the conquest of free knowledge for all students within South Carolina

You can help by reaching out to local policymakers, grouping up with friends and like-minded individuals, attending our in-person campaign forums, and donating

A few things. Do Not Read the contents of this book—says the censor. Do Not Repeat the names of those who came before us—says the revisionist. Do Not Rewrite the past—says all of us.
